About the award

The Annual Coupofy Entrepreneurship Award is aimed at entrepreneurs globally. We are looking for the most interesting and talented entrepreneur in the world to invite for a visit for 1 month at our office space in Bali, Indonesia. All expenses will be covered by us. The winner is welcome to work on a project of their choice while we provide guidance and support.

FAQs

How do I apply?

You will need to submit a short bio, short pitch about your current work project or business, links to social media accounts and why you wish to come to Bali. Please mail to awards@coupofy.com with subject “Award Application Submission”. All applications are due by October 3th, 2015.

Do you need to be actively running a business?

Not necessarily. We are looking for entrepreneurs first. You might be just starting up, transitioning from a corporate job to the start-up world or you might be running an established company. It really doesn’t matter.

Can I bring my team with me?

Yes you can. But we won’t be able to pay for more than 1 person.

When can I come to Bali?

The winner can arrive any day in the year of 2015. However, if you want to experience working alongside our full-time team, we would ideally like to have you in sometime in 2015.
